## 📱 About SOS

### What is SOS?

SOS is a **family safety app** designed to keep you and your loved ones connected and safe. Whether you're checking on your family or responding to an emergency, SOS gives you real-time information and fast communication tools.

Think of it as a **safety net for your family** - it combines emergency alerting, location sharing, family messaging, and useful tools all in one app.

### Why Use SOS?

- ✅ **Emergency Response** - One tap to alert your family in an emergency  
- ✅ **Location Tracking** - Always know where your family members are  
- ✅ **Instant Communication** - Message your family without leaving the app  
- ✅ **Smart Alerts** - Get notified when family members enter or leave safe areas  
- ✅ **Shared Tools** - Track tasks, manage finances, and share memories  
- ✅ **Easy to Use** - Simple design that works for all ages

## 🎯 How to Use

### Getting Started (First Time)

#### **Step 1: Download & Install**
1. Open **App Store** (iPhone) or **Google Play** (Android)
2. Search for "**SOS**"
3. Tap **Install** or **Get**
4. Wait for the app to download

#### **Step 2: Create Your Account**
1. Open the SOS app
2. Tap **Sign Up** or **Create Account**
3. Choose how to sign up:
   - **Email** - Enter email and create password
   - **Phone Number** - Enter phone number
   - **Google** - Use your Google account
   - **Apple ID** - Use your Apple account
4. Verify your identity (email or SMS code)
5. Set up your profile (name, photo, etc.)

#### **Step 3: Enable Permissions**
The app will ask for permission to:
- **Location** - Allow "Always" or "When Using App"
- **Notifications** - Allow notifications
- **Contacts** - Allow access to your phone contacts
- **Phone** - Allow the fake call feature (Android)

✅ **Tip:** Enable all permissions for the best experience. You can change them later in your phone's Settings.

---

### Setting Up Your Family

#### **Option A: Create a New Family**

1. From the home screen, tap **Create Family**
2. Enter a **Family Name** (e.g., "Smith Family Home")
3. Tap **Create**
4. You become the **Admin**
5. You get an **Invite Code** (example: ABC123)
6. **Share this code** with family members by:
   - SMS text message
   - Email
   - Messaging app
   - Verbally in person

#### **Option B: Join an Existing Family**

1. Ask the family admin for their **Invite Code**
2. From the home screen, tap **Join Family**
3. Enter the **6-letter Invite Code**
4. Tap **Join**
5. You're now part of the family! 👨‍👩‍👧

---

### Daily Use - Your First Day

#### **View Your Family on the Map**
1. Tap the **Map** tab (usually 2nd tab)
2. You see a map with your family members as **blue markers**
3. Tap a marker to see their name and details
4. Your own location is a **green marker**
5. If someone sends SOS, their marker turns **red**

#### **Message Your Family**
1. Tap the **Chat** tab (usually 3rd tab)
2. Type your message at the bottom
3. Tap **Send**
4. Messages appear instantly for everyone

#### **Check Family Tasks**
1. From the home screen, find **Tasks**
2. See all family tasks and due dates
3. Tap a task to mark it complete
4. View the family calendar

#### **View Family Members**
1. From the home screen, tap **Family**
2. See a list of all family members
3. Tap a member to see their profile
4. Check their battery level and status

---

### Emergency Use - When You Need Help

#### **Sending an SOS Alert**

1. **Tap and hold the red SOS button** on the home screen
2. Hold it for 2 seconds until it activates
3. **Your location is instantly sent** to all family members
4. They **immediately get a notification** with your location
5. Google Maps link is included so they can find you

#### **What Your Family Sees**
- ✅ Notification: "🚨 [Your Name] needs help!"
- ✅ Your exact location on the map (red marker)
- ✅ Clickable Google Maps link
- ✅ They can call, text, or come help you

#### **Canceling an SOS Alert**
1. Tap the **Cancel** button that appears
2. Family members are notified it was a false alarm
3. Your location goes back to normal (blue marker)

#### **In a Real Emergency**
- ✅ Send SOS (one tap)
- ✅ Call 911 or emergency services
- ✅ Tell your family via the app
- ✅ Enable Ghost Mode if you need to hide

---

### Setting Up Safe Zones

1. Open the app and go to **Safe Zones**
2. Tap **Add Safe Zone**
3. Enter a name (e.g., "Home", "School", "Work")
4. Mark the location on the map
5. Set the **radius** (how far away triggers an alert - usually 100-500 meters)
6. Choose who gets notifications
7. **Save**

Now everyone will get an alert when someone leaves or enters this zone! 🚨

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### **Family & Invites**

**Q: How do I invite family members?**  
A: Go to **Family** > **Share Invite Code**. Send the 6-letter code to family members via text, email, or in person. They enter it in the app to join.

**Q: Can I have multiple families?**  
A: Yes! You can join separate family groups. Switch between them in the **Family** tab.

**Q: What if someone uses a wrong invite code?**  
A: The app will show an error. Make sure they have the correct 6-letter code from the family admin.

**Q: Can I remove someone from the family?**  
A: Yes, admins can remove members. Go to **Family Settings** (admin only).

---

### **Location & Privacy**

**Q: How often is my location updated?**  
A: Location updates automatically **every 5-30 minutes** depending on your settings. Updates happen more frequently when you're moving.

**Q: Can I turn off location sharing?**  
A: Yes! Use **Ghost Mode** to hide your location. Go to **Settings** > **Ghost Mode** > **Turn On**. Family won't see where you are.

**Q: Who can see my location?**  
A: Only family members in your family group can see your location. Non-family members cannot access it.

**Q: Is my location data private?**  
A: Yes! Location data is encrypted and only visible to your family. We don't sell or share your data. See [Privacy & Security](#-privacy--security) below.

---

### **SOS & Emergencies**

**Q: What happens when I send SOS?**  
A: Your location is instantly sent to all family members. They get a notification with a map link to your location. You can cancel it anytime.

**Q: Can I accidentally trigger SOS?**  
A: No. You need to **tap and hold** the SOS button for 2 seconds. It's designed to prevent accidental triggers.

**Q: What's the Fake Call feature?**  
A: Create a fake incoming call that makes it look like someone is calling you. Useful for safety if you're uncomfortable in a situation. User can "answer" the call and leave.

---

### **Safe Zones & Alerts**

**Q: What are Safe Zones?**  
A: Areas you mark as safe (home, school, work, etc.). You get alerts when family members leave or enter these zones.

**Q: How many Safe Zones can I create?**  
A: Unlimited! Create as many as you need.

**Q: Can I mute Safe Zone notifications?**  
A: Yes. Go to **Safe Zone** > **Edit** > Toggle **Notifications Off**.

---

### **General Use**

**Q: What if I lose my phone?**  
A: Log in from another device and disable location tracking. Report the phone to your carrier. For security, remove the lost phone from **Linked Devices** in Settings.

**Q: Can I use SOS without a family?**  
A: You can use most features, but location sharing only works within a family group.

**Q: How do I change my profile picture?**  
A: Go to **Profile** > **Edit** > Tap your photo > Upload new photo.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**  
A: Yes. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Delete Account**. This is permanent.

---

### **Notifications**

**Q: Why am I not getting notifications?**  
A: Check that:
- Notifications are enabled in app settings
- Notifications are enabled in phone settings
- You have internet connection (Wi-Fi or data)

**Q: Can I turn off certain notifications?**  
A: Yes. Go to **Settings** > **Notifications** > Toggle on/off what you want.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### **Location Not Updating**

**Problem:** Location hasn't changed on the map for a while.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check permissions:** Settings > Apps > SOS > Allow Location "Always"
2. **Open the app:** Location updates when the app is open or running in background
3. **Check battery saver:** Disable battery saver mode on your phone
4. **Restart the app:** Close and reopen SOS
5. **Restart your phone:** Turn off and back on
6. **Check internet:** Make sure you have Wi-Fi or mobile data

✅ **Note:** Location updates every 5-30 minutes—it won't update constantly.

---

### **Notifications Not Working**

**Problem:** You're not getting alerts or messages.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check app notifications:** Settings > Apps > SOS > Allow Notifications
2. **Check phone notifications:** Go to phone Settings > Notifications > SOS > Allow
3. **Restart app:** Close and fully reopen the app
4. **Check Do Not Disturb:** Make sure Do Not Disturb is off
5. **Check internet:** Need Wi-Fi or mobile data for notifications
6. **Reinstall app:** Delete and reinstall SOS

✅ **Note:** You need internet for push notifications to work.

---

### **Can't Find Family Members**

**Problem:** Family member not showing up on the map.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check invite code:** Make sure they used the correct code
2. **Check their app:** They must have the app open or running in background
3. **Check their location:** They might have Ghost Mode on
4. **Check permissions:** They need location permission enabled
5. **Refresh the map:** Pull down to refresh, wait 30 seconds
6. **Check internet:** Make sure everyone has data/Wi-Fi

✅ **Note:** Location updates take a few minutes to appear.

---

### **App Crashes or Won't Open**

**Problem:** App closes when you try to use it.

**Solutions:**
1. **Force stop:** Go to Settings > Apps > SOS > Force Stop
2. **Clear cache:** Settings > Apps > SOS > Storage > Clear Cache
3. **Restart phone:** Turn off and back on
4. **Reinstall:** Delete app and reinstall from store
5. **Check storage:** Make sure you have 100+ MB free space
6. **Update OS:** Install latest Android/iOS updates
7. **Contact support:** If issue continues, reach out to support

✅ **Note:** Clearing cache won't delete your account or data.

---

### **Battery Drain Issues**

**Problem:** App uses too much battery.

**Solutions:**
1. **Stop background location:** Settings > Location > Turn off Background tracking
2. **Reduce update frequency:** Settings > Location > Update every 30 min (instead of 5)
3. **Enable Ghost Mode:** Hide location temporarily
4. **Enable battery saver:** Phone's battery saver mode
5. **Close age apps:** Close other apps running in background
6. **Restart phone:** Turn off and back on

✅ **Note:** Location tracking naturally uses battery. It's normal.
